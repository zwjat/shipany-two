# Refund Application

## Request a Refund for howold.io Age Detection Service

If you are not completely satisfied with our AI age detection service, you may be eligible for a refund. Please review our [Refund Policy](/refund-policy) for eligibility criteria before submitting your request.

### Required Information

To process your refund application, please provide the following details:

- **Full Name**
- **Email Address** associated with your account
- **Order Number** or transaction ID
- **Purchase Date**
- **Plan Type** (Basic Pack, Value Pack, Standard Monthly, Pro Monthly, Standard Annual, Pro Annual)
- **Number of Analyses Used** (must be less than 20% of purchased analyses)
- **Reason for Refund Request**
- **Details** about any technical issues or accuracy concerns

### How to Apply for a Refund

Please send your refund request to our support team with all the required information:

**Email:** [refund@howold.io](mailto:refund@howold.io)

### Processing Timeline

- **Response Time:** Our support team will acknowledge your request within 24 hours
- **Review Period:** Refund applications are typically processed within 2-3 business days
- **Refund Processing:** Approved refunds are processed within 3-5 business days

### Important Notes

- Refunds are only available for unused analyses (less than 20% used)
- Requests must be made within 14 days of purchase for most plans
- Annual subscription refunds have special conditions (full refund within first 30 days)
- Free trial analyses (5 free analyses) are non-refundable
- Please allow additional time for the refund to appear in your account based on your payment provider's processing times

### Plan-Specific Refund Guidelines

- **One-time Packs (Basic/Value):** Refundable if less than 20% of analyses used
- **Monthly Subscriptions:** Cancel anytime, refund for current period if eligible
- **Annual Subscriptions:** Full refund within 30 days, pro-rated after 30 days
- **Used Analyses:** Once age analyses have been processed, they are non-refundable
